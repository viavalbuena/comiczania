Starlight

Starlight is revealed through the eyes of 'Margaret', as she relives childhood fears of the dark that awoke her curiosity about the night sky. Join Margaret as she takes you on a journey of discovery about constellations and ancient cultures, and the lives of stars. Produced by Melbourne Planetarium. Narrated by Australian actress Sigrid Thornton. Approximately 45 minutes, inclusive of night sky tour.


Bookings are strongly recommended. Phone the booking office on 3403 2578.
