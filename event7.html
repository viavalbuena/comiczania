Character Encounters at Star Wars Launch Bay Courtyard

Seize the chance to encounter popular Characters from the Star Wars universe at this Tomorrowland hub.


Darth Vader

Anticipate an epic run-in with the mysterious Dark Lord of the Sith.

Be ever so mindful as you step over to the dark side and encounter this imposing enforcer of the Galactic Empire. This menacing side of the Force can be alluring, but remember… it’s ultimately up to you to choose your fate.

Chewbacca

Get ready for a rebel rendezvous with Han Solo’s loyal comrade and Millennium Falcon copilot.

This immense Wookiee from Kashyyyk may be known for his towering stature and great strength, but you may discover that a quick wit and a big heart are his most memorable qualities.
