ABOUT




Blast off to infinity and beyond, cruise the galaxy in a Starspeeder and meet some of your favorite characters from a galaxy far, far away!

ComicZania represents the vision and fantasy of futurists such as H.G. Wells, Jules Verne and, of course, Walt Disney. Guests can zoom through Space Mountain, travel through the galaxy in Star Tours, drive racecars and enjoy so much more as they experience the “future”.
