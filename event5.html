Epcot International Festival of the Arts

Don’t miss this winter gem –– a thoroughly original festival experience merging a trifecta of artistry into one: performing, culinary and visual arts. Promising to captivate, this experience will feature opportunities to sample artisanal cuisine with a Disney flair at a variety of inventive food stations. Guests will soak up curated art exhibits and also have the opportunity to participate in exclusive art workshops. Topping things off, festival-goers will be treated to magnificent performances from an array of Broadway stars, jazz ensembles, marching bands and more!


What happens when you mix Disney with visual, culinary and performing arts inspired from all around the world? You get the fourth annual Epcot® International Festival of the Arts, running Jan. 17 – Feb. 24, 2020. And this year is really exciting, because you can enjoy the Disney on Broadway Concert Series seven days a week.

Experience thrilling performances during the Disney on Broadway Concert Series at the America Gardens Theatre. The stage lights up nightly and features some of the brightest stars on Broadway performing beloved Disney songs from their award-winning shows. Plus discover a potpourri of live entertainment across the Park, including Art in Performance! at the World Showcase Plaza Stage. It’s a high-energy, onstage creation by a guest visual artist painting favorite Disney Characters — in just minutes, watch colorful Disney images magically appear!

As part of Park admission, there are so many artful sights, surprises and unique experiences to explore:

Grab a paintbrush and help bring the giant paint-by-number mural at World Showcase Plaza to life!

Learn how to draw some of your favorite Characters from Disney artists at the Animation Academy (offered multiple times each day)

In our Artful Photo Ops, you can step into some of the most famous art from around the world and become part of the painting — capture the moment with your own camera or with the help of one of our Disney PhotoPass® Photographers.

Discover amazing art galleries throughout World Showcase. Enjoy artful interpretations of Disney Characters. Explore intriguing art techniques of cultures near and far. Purchase favorite works. And so much more.

For those with an appetite for the culinary arts, you can feast on artistic dishes with a delicious Disney twist like the Artist Palette Jumbo Chocolate Chip Cookie. Sample art-inspired menus at the many Food Studios. And find lots of other fun food diversions to sink your teeth into.

And new this year, don’t miss the Wonderful Walk of Colorful Cuisine — a technicolor tour that allows you to enjoy a vibrant array of delicious dishes as you stroll the festival — culminating in a colorful culinary prize.
